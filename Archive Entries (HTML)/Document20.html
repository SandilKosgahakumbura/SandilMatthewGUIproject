Archive of Our Own

Archive of Our Own (often shortened to AO3) is a nonprofit open source repository for fanfiction and other fanworks contributed by users. The site was created in 2008 by the Organization for Transformative Works and went into open beta in 2009 and continues to be in beta.[2] As of 1 May 2024, Archive of Our Own hosts 12,900,000 works in over 64,860 fandoms including those related to real people.[1] The site has received generally positive reception for its curation, organization, and design, mostly done by readers and writers of fanfiction.[3][4] (Sourced from en.wikipedia.org)

We used Ao3 as a reference for our website as a archival site. The end result was wildly different and by looking at the way this site is structured I tried to emulate some of the function of the website to limited success, The website Uses librararies and frameworks that go the basic options that bootstrap could offer so for certain aspects We were forced to use out of the box methods to emulate the layout somewhat accurately. -Sandil Kosgahakumbura
